Unchanged Philosophy

  • Less is more - The core still remains small and handy, only 3K minimized. Yet it can be extended and customized to included more features easily.
  • Separation of Concern - Unobtrusive JavaScript, content independent Grid system, content & template in HTML.
  • Convention over configuration - Convention is advised but not enforced, you can follow the defaults and have a nicely working system, or customize radically and build your super site.
  • It is easier to make simple things better than making better things simple - It's still simple, and ways to make it better are included.

The target of this framework is to provide a handy tool for easy content integration. Yet I believe it is powerful enough to serve as cornerstone for more advanced portals.
